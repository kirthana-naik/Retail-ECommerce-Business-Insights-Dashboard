📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

This project is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze and monitor E-Commerce business performance.

The dashboard is designed as a Control Room for decision-makers, providing a complete view of revenue, profitability, customer segments, regional performance, product categories, and sales trends.

🎯 Business Objectives
• Track overall business performance through key KPIs.
• Analyze revenue and profit trends across different regions.
• Understand customer purchasing behavior and segment contribution.
• Identify top-performing products and categories.
• Evaluate payment method preferences.
• Monitor profit margins and discount impact on profitability.
• Support data-driven business decisions.

📈 Key Insights
• Consumer segment generated the highest revenue contribution.
• Europe emerged as the top-performing region.
• Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
• October recorded the highest monthly sales.
• Furniture category generated the highest revenue.
• Higher discounts showed a direct impact on profitability.

✅ Outcome
Developed an interactive dashboard that transforms raw E-Commerce data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders monitor performance, identify opportunities, and improve strategic decision-making.
